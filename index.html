Who we are

Waqiatul Cares Foundation is a non-governmental, nonprofit-making organization deeply committed to the care, protection, and overall well-being of older and elderly individuals within society. Recognizing the unique challenges faced by this vulnerable group, the organization provides essential support services that promote healthy aging, social inclusion, and emotional well-being. In addition to offering direct care, the organization actively advocates against all forms of violence, abuse, neglect, and harmful practices targeted at the elderly. It seeks to raise public awareness about the rights and dignity of older people and to influence policies that safeguard their interests. Driven by a vision of a peaceful, inclusive, and compassionate society, the organization empowers the elderly to lead self-reliant and meaningful lives. Through its programs and initiatives, it aims to foster development and create an environment where every individual, regardless of age, can thrive and attain their full potential.
